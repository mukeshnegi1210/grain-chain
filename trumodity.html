Comprehensive Transaction Platform

Trumodity is GrainChain's comprehensive transaction platform for the agricultural industry. Cmodity facilitates prompt payment to Producers and suppliers and the immediate availability of tradable commodities to buyers. It attacks fraud and corruption through certification and accountability while streamlining procedures that burden the marketplace and its participants. Cmodity's innovative blockchain architecture creates a secure, immutable record of commodity data and transactions along the entire supply chain.

Mobile App Features

  • Blockchain-enabled platform with enhanced security, reliability, transparency and data privacy
  • Fast domestic and international settlements using GrainPay, including instant title transfers
  • Easily negotiate complex purchase agreements with multiple parties using smart contracts
