Helping Grain Elevators, Producers and Drivers Work Together in a Better, Faster, and More Secure Way

Challenges For Storage Operators

  • How do storage operators overcome the limitations and challenges of inventory management based around manual entry and paper records?
  • How do we get harvests from farm to silo in the quickest and most efficient way possible?
  • How can we facilitate faster transfer of title and payments for commodities?
  • The answer to all of these questions is GrainChain's suite of products.

Provides a comprehensive transaction platform for producers and buyers

Benefits of the Trumodity Transaction Platform

  • Reduces uncertainty and cycles of debt as Producers receive payments more quickly and with more payment options.
  • Smart contracts guarantee reliable and efficient transactions. They are executed automatically with no additional intervention needed, eliminating costs and delays.
  • Easily create complex, multi-party contracts utilizing online tools.
  • Facilitates non-local and international market access to small or medium size Producers.
  • Producers can hedge using forward contracts and optional insurance products.
  • Ability to track commodities from the field enables lenders to open up financing for growers.
  • All data is securely and accurately stored on the blockchain, eliminating waste and fraud.
  • Manage your transactions and data from your mobile device.

Building security, transparency and efficiency into our solutions

GrainChain’s suite of products work together to improve efficiency, reliability and accountability to every stage of the commodities supply chain.

Trumodity facilitates smart contracts between Producers and buyers and ensures accurate, real-time tracking of commodities throughout the supply chain. It ensures prompt payment for Producers and immediate transfer of ownership for buyers.

SiloSys is our inventory management platform that securely records and tracks commodity data from farm to grain elevator to buyer. Data from Trumodity and Silosys feeds into the blockchain to ensure security, transparency and accountability.

HarvX is our logistics platform that facilitates efficient pickup and delivery of commodities from harvest to grain elevator to buyers. The movement of inventory into and out of facilities can be fully tracked through SiloSys and Trumodity.
